Privacy Policy for MyKiosk Lite

Effective Date: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyKiosk Lite ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

MyKiosk Lite does not collect or store personal data such as names, emails, or phone numbers.

However, the app uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information.

  • No personal account information is required to use the app
  • No location data is directly collected by us
  • No personal identity data is stored by the app

2. Data Storage

All inventory and sales data entered into the app is stored locally on the user's device.

This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains under the user's control.

3. Third-Party Services

MyKiosk Lite uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and process certain data automatically, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage data

This data is used to provide and improve advertising services.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not share user-entered data with third parties.

However, third-party services such as AdMob may process data as described above.

5. Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally, users are responsible for securing their device.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. User Choices

Users can control ad personalization through their device settings or Google Ad Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
