Privacy Policy for MyKiosk Lite

Effective Date: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyKiosk Lite ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

MyKiosk Lite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No device tracking is performed

2. Data Storage

All data entered into the app (such as inventory items and sales records) is stored locally on the user’s device.

This data is not transmitted to any external servers and remains under the user's control.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties because no data is collected or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

MyKiosk Lite does not use third-party services that collect or process user data.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, users are responsible for securing their device against unauthorized access.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
